Know what to do

Watch for symptoms

  • Frequent watery diarrhea
  • Loss of appetite or weight
  • Cramping, bloating, nausea
  • Fatigue and low-grade fever

Reduce risk

  • Wash hands before food prep
  • Rinse produce under running water
  • Scrub firm produce with a clean brush
  • Refrigerate cut produce promptly

Get care

Contact a healthcare provider for sudden or persistent diarrhea. Testing may require multiple stool specimens. The usual treatment is trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole; a clinician should guide treatment.
